about us

HANI Models is a boutique women’s modelling agency representing talent across fashion, beauty and lifestyle.

We work with models selected for their aesthetic strength, presence and individuality, supporting both editorial and image-driven commercial projects.

Our roster includes women of varied backgrounds and styles, united by clarity of image and the ability to move naturally across different visual moods — from soft and elegant to bold and expressive — while maintaining a strong sense of identity.

There is no single “HANI look.” What connects our models is presence: the ability to hold attention with simplicity, intention and authenticity.

We represent talent with long-term visual potential and a refined, adaptable image suited to contemporary fashion and beauty work.
